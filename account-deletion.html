⚠️ Important Notice

Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted:

  • You will lose access to all features and services
  • Your card collection, scan history, and saved data will be permanently deleted
  • Your subscription will be cancelled (you must also cancel through Google Play Store)
  • Your username will become available for others to use
  • This action cannot be undone

How to Request Account Deletion

📱 Method 1: Delete Account In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open the TradingCard App on your device
  2. Go to Settings / Profile tab
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. You will receive a verification code via email
  5. Enter the code to confirm deletion
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted

📧 Method 2: Request via Email

Send an email to trading-card-scaner.@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account with:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - [Your Username]
  • Body: Include your username and the email address associated with your account
  • We will send a confirmation email to verify your identity
  • Once verified, your account will be deleted within 7 business days

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

Data Type Will Be Deleted Retention Period
Account information (username, email, password hash) ✅ Yes Immediately upon deletion
Card collection data ✅ Yes Immediately upon deletion
Scan history and results ✅ Yes Immediately upon deletion
Profile information (avatar, settings) ✅ Yes Immediately upon deletion
Subscription status ✅ Yes Immediately upon deletion
Usage analytics (anonymized) ❌ No Retained anonymously for up to 90 days
Error logs ❌ No Automatically deleted after 30 days
Backup data (if applicable) ✅ Yes Within 30 days after account deletion

Data That May Be Retained

In certain circumstances, we may need to retain some data even after account deletion:

  • Legal compliance: If required by applicable law or regulation
  • Fraud prevention: If necessary to investigate or prevent fraud
  • Anonymized analytics: Aggregated, non-personal data may be retained for analytical purposes
  • Financial records: Transaction records may be kept for tax/accounting purposes as required by law

How to Cancel Your Subscription

Account deletion does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. You must also:

  1. Open Google Play Store on your device
  2. Tap on your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions
  3. Select Subscriptions
  4. Find TradingCard App and tap Cancel subscription
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm
